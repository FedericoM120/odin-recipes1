Steak

Steak contains protein which is an essential building block of the human body. Therefore it can be safely assumed that steak is essential to the human body. Steak also happens to taste really good but if you happen to not like steak that is okay too.

Ingredients:

  • New York Strip Steak
  • crushed pepper
  • salt
  • butter
  • garlic
  • rosemarry
  • oil

Recipe:

  1. Pat dry the steak
  2. Season generously with salt and pepper
  3. Preheat the pan and brush with oil
  4. Sear the steak on each side for 3-4 minutes
  5. add butter, garlic, and rosemarry
  6. remove the steak and rest 10 minutes before slicing against the grain
