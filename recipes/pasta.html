Pasta

Pasta is one of the oldest foods on earth. Some people say that kings and Queens only ate pasta because it gave them special powers.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Parsley
  • lemon
  • Parmesan cheese

Pasta Recipe:

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Toast the garlic and red pepper flakes
  3. stir in the past with the lemon juice
  4. Garnish with parmesan cheese
